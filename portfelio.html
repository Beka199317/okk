Ice cream before (left image) and after completion of run (right image) with air added from churning --overrun-- estimate change in volume is about 20%. Overrun is the measurement of air that is whipped into the ice cream and is calculated as the percentage increase in volume of the finished ice cream. Many commercial ice cream can have an overrun of 75-100%, however super premium ice creams may be as low as 20%.
